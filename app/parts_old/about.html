AUROOM LIGHTING SYSTEM

Beautiful, noble, sophisticated and elegant lighting system made of 100% brass. In the entire history of lighting, no manufacturer has been able to create anything like this system. AUROOM is a combination of two words: “aura” and “room”, and "aurum" has an additional meaning: gold.

100% BRASS

Products made of genuine brass have always been valued throughout history, only gold and silver products had greater value. The special brass alloy used in manufacturing of Auroom allows to receive the noble shade of gold, as well as sophisticated black and steel, and trendy pink for AUROOM PINK collection.

MADE BY HAND

We use specialized cutting-edge molecular treatment technologies for brass to produce the unique color, all components are made of brass, sanded and treated by hand.
