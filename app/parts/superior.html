Beautiful, noble, sophisticated and elegant lighting system made of 100% brass. In the entire history of lighting, no manufacturer has been able to create anything like this system. AUROOM is a combination of two words: “aura” and “room”, and "aurum" has an additional meaning: gold.

DIMA LOGINOFF, a world-renown designer, who creates designs for CARTIER, has created a capsule collection of luminaires specially for Auroom lighting system. "A little irony and mischief."

KARIM RASHID, a world-famous industrial designer from New York: "I created unique designs for AUROOM lighting system using pure geometry married with organic forms with function being always inseparable. My desire was to materialize the immaterial ‘light’. Sensual Minimalism". Globalove, Karim
